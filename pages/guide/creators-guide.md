# Creators Guide

## Step 1. Login and Name Your Store

Log in to your account. You'll see the My Store page where you can set your store name and connect a social account for verification.

![My Store page before verification](/assets/image-54.png)

## Step 2. Connect Social Account

Connect your Google or X account to verify your identity. Once connected, the "New Product" button becomes active.

![My Store page after Google verification](/assets/image-53.png)

## Step 3. Upload Your First Blob

Click "New Product" to create your first listing.

![New Product upload form](/assets/image-14.png)

1. Choose a name and set the price for your Blob.
2. Write a description that highlights what makes your Blob special.
3. Upload a cover image (up to 5 images, max 2MB each, 1024×1024px recommended).
4. Upload your product file (up to 512MB) or provide a download URL.
5. Publish your Blob and start earning!
