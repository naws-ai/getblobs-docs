# GetBlobs Overview

![GetBlobs hero - desktop and mobile](/assets/image-11.png)

[GetBlobs](https://getblobs.com/) is an online platform for creators to upload, share, and trade digital content. By leveraging P2P crypto payments, GetBlobs offers a seamless and creator-first approach to monetizing digital assets without traditional payment processes.

## What is a Blob?

"Blob" stands for "Binary Large Object," a term used in computer science to describe large chunks of data like images, audio files, videos, and 3D models.

At GetBlobs, the concept of "Blob" is expanded to include all forms of creative digital assets — representing the unique ideas and creativity of their creators.
