# Core Features

## Digital Content Trading

GetBlobs empowers creators with an intuitive platform for showcasing and monetizing their work. Creators can upload and trade digital assets spanning audio files, images, videos, and 3D models. The streamlined upload interface ensures anyone can start selling their creations with ease.

## P2P Crypto Payments

The platform leverages P2P crypto payments, allowing direct transactions without intermediaries or high fees. Automated crypto swaps support multiple cryptocurrencies, enabling smooth transactions between buyers and sellers using their preferred currencies.

## NAWS Payment System

GetBlobs incorporates the NAWS crypto payment system for flexibility and security. Features include:

- Automatic multi-crypto swap between various cryptocurrencies
- Real-time value adjustments based on live market rates
- Blockchain-recorded transactions for full transparency
- Decentralized authentication for user data protection

## Global Reach

GetBlobs eliminates geographical and currency barriers through blockchain technology, enabling effortless international transactions. Creators can connect with a global audience without limitations.
